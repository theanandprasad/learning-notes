3 Prompts to "Fix Your Life": Identity → Value → Revenue

Three big LLM "mega-prompts" chained into one arc: first find your identity, then make it valuable in an AI world, then turn it into revenue.

⚠️ Captured as-is, distilled. The "fix your entire life in 1 day" framing is hyperbole, and the article ends by selling the author's own $5 X community ("seven c pirate crew"). The prompts are his creative product — this note distills each prompt's framework and key outputs rather than reproducing the full copy-paste text (read the source for that). He credits the whole idea to a Dan Koe post (Koe said don't use AI; the author disagrees), and notes each prompt is meant to be paired with a source article you paste in.

The arc

A three-step pipeline you run through an LLM: (1) engineer your identity (change the foundation, not the behavior), (2) build a "trust moat" so you stay valuable as AI makes information free, and (3) monetize that identity + moat through a tiered ladder. Each prompt's output (an evolving "Video Game Map" of your life) feeds the next.

Prompt 1 — Identity & Vision (the foundation)

Role: a "Master Identity Strategist & Cybernetic Coach." Core philosophy: "discipline is a symptom of a rotting foundation" — real change is first-order (shift identity), not second-order (force behavior). It runs on a Cybernetic loop (Act → Sense → Compare → Iterate) and the Teleological principle: all behavior, even procrastination, is a goal-oriented attempt to protect your current identity. (If you set a goal like "be more productive," the prompt is told to stop you and re-route to identity questions.)

Prompt 2 — Value (the trust moat)

Role: a "Digital Sovereign Strategist." Mission: keep you from becoming "a couch potato on UBI" as AI and robotics take over intelligence and labor — by building a Trust Moat, the only value that survives when the cost of creating information drops to zero. It interrogates the Game Map from Prompt 1 and shifts you from production to verification & synthesis.

Prompt 3 — Revenue (the engine)

Role: a "Sovereign Monetization Architect." It turns your identity shift + trust moat into a tiered ecosystem that sells Transformation, Convenience, and Access.
