The Real Reason You're Not Growing on CT

By AR (@tenacious_ar) • Published Apr 15, 2026 • Saved Jun 8, 2026
Audience Growth Crypto Twitter Personal Brand Consistency

"X is like a CV of Web3." Growing your account matters — but the real reason you're stuck isn't tactics. It's motivation: you're treating it as a slot machine instead of something you enjoy.

ℹ️ Note: This is Crypto-Twitter ("CT") growth advice from a Web3 creator; the earnings figures near the end ($50–$4,000 per post) are the author's casual claims, captured as-is — not verified, not financial advice. The mindset core, though, generalizes to any audience-building.

The reframe

You don't need motivation to open Instagram or TikTok — they're entertainment. You join CT for money (alpha, wins, opportunities), so the moment you go 2–3 months without earning, you lose steam. Fix the mindset first: "I'm here to interact with people, build connections, and learn." Let earning be the second outcome, not the main one — then the consistency that actually grows an account becomes possible.

1 · GM posts — CT culture; works well for engagement.
2 · Upcoming projects / early alpha — share what you're seeing.
3 · Your take on trends — CT or outside CT.
4 · Interactive posts — ask your audience questions.
