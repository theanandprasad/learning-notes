The Real Reason You're Not Growing on Crypto Twitter

By AR (@tenacious_ar) • Published Apr 15, 2026 • Saved Jun 9, 2026
Crypto Twitter Mindset Consistency Replies

X is "the CV of Web3" — but most people approach it earning-first, lose motivation in 2–3 months, and quit. The fix is a mindset shift before any tactic: enjoy the platform, and let earning be the second outcome.

⚠️ Captured as-is. A mindset-first growth piece for Crypto Twitter; earnings figures ($50–$4,000/post) are the author's own, and it ends with a share CTA. One creator's perspective (his niche is NFTs), not financial or guaranteed advice.

The real reason

Two things attract people: entertainment and money. You open Instagram/TikTok for fun and never need motivation. You open X (CT) expecting alpha, wins, and opportunities — it's all earning. So when you don't earn for 2–3 months, motivation dies. The reframe: "I'm using X to interact with people, build connections, and learn new things." Let earning be the second outcome, not the main one — then you won't quit.

The basics: set your profile

Content: post 3–4× a day, in your niche

Stay active and consistent; find a niche you enjoy and know. If you run out of ideas, his four:

1 · GM posts. CT culture — works well for engagement.
2 · Share upcoming projects / early alpha (in his case, NFT projects).
3 · Share your thoughts on trends — CT or outside CT.
4 · Make interactive posts. Ask your audience questions.

Being a reply guy — the right way

Quality replies keep you visible on the TL. Quality content builds your identity.

How you reply matters more than that you reply. You can use lists, reply randomly, engage only with people you follow, or turn on notifications for specific accounts — but the best way is simple: engage with what you genuinely like, naturally.

The @MedusaOnchain example

People dissect her "strategy," but he's watched her since ~2K followers: she always interacted genuinely — her old replies and current ones are the same. She enjoys it and is passionate about it. That's what grew her. Enjoy the process and you stay consistent; chase a result and you'll feel disappointed and lose motivation.

Discipline is the whole game

Magical things only happen when you are disciplined.

Create content and write quality replies every day, and there will be a day a post goes viral and brings the right people to you. Either you grow slowly, brick by brick, or suddenly one piece of content reaches the audience you've been working toward. Stay consistent and you'll get there.
