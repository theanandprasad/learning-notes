The Real Reason You're Not Growing on Crypto Twitter

By AR (@tenacious_ar) • Published Apr 15, 2026 • Saved Jun 9, 2026
Crypto Twitter Mindset Consistency Replies

"X is like a CV of Web3." Most people stay inconsistent on Crypto Twitter — and the real reason isn't tactics, it's motivation: you joined to earn, and earning is a slow, fragile thing to run on.

⚠️ Captured as-is. A mindset piece for Crypto Twitter; the earnings figures at the end are the author's own estimates and end with a share CTA. One creator's perspective (his niche is NFTs).

The mindset flip

Two things attract people: entertainment and money. You scroll Instagram/TikTok for fun and never need motivation. You open X for alpha and wins — pure earning. But if earning is your only intention and you don't earn for 2–3 months, you'll lose motivation. The fix: "I'm using X to interact with people, build connections, and learn new things." Let earning be the second outcome, not the main one.

Set up the profile

Create content (3–4× a day)

Stay active and consistent, and find a niche you enjoy and know (his is NFTs). If you run out of ideas, his four:

1 · GM posts. It's CT culture and works well for engagement.
2 · Upcoming projects / early alpha. Share what's coming in your niche.
3 · Your thoughts on trends — inside CT or outside it.
4 · Interactive posts. Ask your audience questions.

On being a reply guy

"Quality replies keep you visible on the TL. Quality content builds your identity." You can use lists, reply randomly, engage only people you follow, or turn on notifications for specific accounts — but the best way is simpler: engage with what you genuinely like, naturally.

His example: people study @MedusaOnchain's "strategy," but he's watched her since ~2K followers — she always interacted genuinely, and nothing changed as she grew. She enjoys it and is passionate; that's what made her grow. "If you enjoy the process, you'll stay consistent. If you're chasing a specific result, you'll often feel disappointed and eventually lose motivation."

Discipline is the whole game

Magical things only happen when you are disciplined.

What it leads to

Grow 0 → 4–5K followers naturally and opportunities start: $50–$200 deals; as your influence grows, up to $4,000 per post as a bigger creator — working with agencies, individuals, and projects. (Figures are the author's own.)
